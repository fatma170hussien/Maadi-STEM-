Classroom Layout & Dimensions

15m × 8m collaborative learning space designed for 25 students with 4 group tables

Updated Dimensions

Each classroom measures 15m (length) × 8m (width) with a ceiling height of 3.2m, providing 120m² of spacious learning environment.

Student Capacity & Layout

Maximum capacity of 25 students arranged in 4 collaborative group tables with 6-7 students per group, promoting teamwork and discussion.

Interactive Whiteboard

Front-mounted 4K interactive whiteboard with touch capability, positioned for optimal visibility from all student seating areas.

Collaborative Design

Group table arrangement encourages peer learning, with ample space for movement and teacher circulation throughout the classroom.
