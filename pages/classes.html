Interactive Learning

Modern classrooms designed for presentations, discussions, and creative activities

Our Approach

Three pillars of modern classroom learning at Maadi STEM

Presentation Skills

Students regularly present projects using smart boards and digital tools, building confidence and communication skills.

Discussion-Based

Circular seating arrangements encourage active participation, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Creative Activities

Art projects, creative writing, and design thinking integrated into all subjects for holistic learning.

Presentation Skills

Developing confident communicators through technology

Student Presentations & Smart Technology

Our classrooms feature interactive smart boards where students present their research, projects, and innovative ideas. This regular practice builds essential skills for future leaders.

  • Weekly Presentations - Every student presents regularly
  • Digital Tools - Using PowerPoint, Prezi, and other software
  • Peer Feedback - Constructive criticism from classmates
  • Public Speaking - Building confidence in front of audiences

From science projects to art critiques, presentations are integral to our learning culture.

Classroom Layout & Dimensions

Our classrooms are designed for optimal interaction and collaboration, with flexible seating arrangements for various learning activities.

Room Dimensions

Each classroom measures 15m × 8m (120m² total area), providing ample space for 25 students with comfortable movement and activity areas.

Seating Arrangement

Four group tables accommodate 6-7 students each, arranged in a U-shape to facilitate discussions and maintain eye contact with the teacher.

Teaching Space

Front area includes an interactive whiteboard and teacher's desk positioned for optimal visibility and engagement with all students.

Accessibility

Wide aisles between groups and multiple exit points ensure easy movement and comply with all safety regulations for educational spaces.

Engaging activities that make learning interactive and fun

Classroom

A bright and neatly arranged classroom designed to provide a comfortable and focused learning environment. The organized desks, chairs, and soft natural light create a calm atmosphere ideal for effective teaching and productive study sessions.

Art & Creativity

Integrating artistic expression with STEM education

Creative Expression in STEM

We believe creativity is essential for innovation. Our classrooms include art activities that complement STEM learning:

  • Science Art - Drawing biological diagrams and chemical structures
  • Math Designs - Creating geometric patterns and mathematical art
  • Engineering Models - Building creative prototypes and designs
  • Tech Design - Digital art and graphic design projects

Art helps students visualize abstract concepts and develop innovative thinking skills.

Technology Integration

Preparing students for a digital future

Digital Learning Environment

Every student uses a personal laptop as a primary learning tool, developing essential digital skills for the 21st century.

  • Online Research - Access to global information resources
  • Coding Practice - Hands-on computer programming
  • Digital Notes - Organized electronic note-taking
  • Virtual Labs - Simulations and virtual experiments

Technology enhances learning without replacing traditional methods, creating a balanced educational experience.
