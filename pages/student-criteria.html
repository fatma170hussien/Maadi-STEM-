Student Admission Criteria

Discover the requirements and procedures for joining Egypt's prestigious STEM schools in 2025

Admission Requirements 2025

To join Egypt's Excellence STEM Schools, students must meet specific academic, health, and age requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Egyptian nationality only
  • Middle school certificate completion
  • Age must not exceed 18 years by October 1, 2025
  • Good health condition with medical clearance
  • No chronic, infectious, or psychological diseases

Academic Excellence

  • Minimum overall grade of 98% (274.5 marks)
  • OR 95% (266 marks) with perfect score in at least one subject
  • Perfect score subjects: Science, Mathematics, or English
  • First-round completion of basic education (2024/2025)
  • Middle school graduation certificate

Admission Tests

  • English subject
  • Mathematics subject
  • Science subject
  • IQ test
  • From 4th grade to 9th grade content

Important Notice

All applications must be submitted electronically through the Ministry of Education's official website only. Paper applications are not accepted. The application fee is approximately 250 EGP, payable through designated channels. Late submissions will not be considered under any circumstances.

Application Timeline

Follow these steps to complete your application successfully

Step 1: Online Registration

Register electronically through the Ministry of Education's official website during the specified application period. Complete all required fields accurately.

1

Step 2: Document Submission

Upload required documents: personal photo, success statement, birth certificate, and payment receipt (approx. 250 EGP). Print and sign the application form.

2

Step 3: Physical Submission

Submit the printed application form with certified documents to the relevant educational administration office for verification and processing.

3

Step 4: Aptitude Tests

Take electronic aptitude tests covering logical thinking, mental readiness, mathematics, science, and English language at designated test centers.

4

Step 5: Interviews & Results

Successful candidates proceed to personal interviews. Final admission results are announced on the official website and through educational administrations.

5
