What is a Fab Lab?

A Fabrication Laboratory (Fab Lab) is a technical prototyping platform for innovation and invention, providing stimulus for local entrepreneurship. Our Fab Lab is a digital fabrication workshop equipped with an array of flexible computer-controlled tools that cover several different length scales and various materials.

At Maadi STEM School, our Fab Lab serves as the central hub for capstone projects, where students transform theoretical knowledge into practical solutions through hands-on manufacturing and prototyping.

The lab supports the entire design cycle from concept to production, enabling students to design, prototype, and test their innovations in a professional-grade environment.

Safety Guidelines

Ensuring a safe working environment for all Fab Lab users

Eye Protection

Safety goggles must be worn at all times when operating any machinery or handling materials.

Protective Clothing

Lab coats and closed-toe shoes are mandatory. Loose clothing and jewelry must be secured.

Training Required

All users must complete safety training and equipment-specific certification before use.

Supervision

Advanced equipment requires supervision by trained staff or certified student leaders.
