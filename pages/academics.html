Academic Excellence

A rigorous STEM curriculum that develops critical thinkers and innovative problem-solvers for Egypt's future

Essential Skills Development

Beyond textbooks - we cultivate the complete innovator through immersive skill-building experiences

Presentation & Communication

Master the art of persuasive communication through Harkness pedagogy, where students lead discussions around oval tables. Weekly presentations, debate sessions, and public speaking opportunities build confidence and clarity in expressing complex STEM concepts.

Students learn to create compelling narratives around their projects, present to expert panels, and defend their solutions with evidence-based reasoning.

Collaboration & Teamwork

In our project-based learning environment, teamwork isn't just encouragedit's essential. Students work in diverse teams to tackle real-world challenges, learning to leverage individual strengths, manage conflicts, and achieve collective goals.

Through semester-long capstone projects, students experience the dynamics of professional teamwork, preparing them for university research groups and future careers.

Critical Thinking & Problem Solving

Biweekly journal exams challenge students to reflect deeply on their work, analyze complex problems, and develop innovative solutions. We teach students not just what to think, but how to thinkapplying logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and creative problem-solving to Egypt's Grand Challenges.

Every project begins with "Why?" and progresses through rigorous analysis before reaching innovative solutions.

Research & Inquiry

Students become independent researchers, learning to formulate hypotheses, design experiments, collect data, and draw evidence-based conclusions. Using college-level references from day one, they develop academic rigor and intellectual curiosity.

From literature reviews to experimental design, students experience the complete research cycle, preparing them for university-level work and beyond.

Technical & Digital Literacy

Beyond traditional STEM subjects, students gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies. From programming and robotics in our Fab Lab to data analysis and 3D modeling, we ensure our graduates are technologically fluent and adaptable.

Students don't just use technologythey understand how it works and how to create with it, preparing them for rapidly evolving digital landscapes.

Capstone Projects

Applying classroom knowledge to solve Egypt's grand challenges through innovation and engineering

What is Capstone?

Each semester, students work in teams to tackle one of Egypt's 11 Grand Challenges using the Engineering Design Process (EDP). These projects integrate knowledge from all subject areas and result in testable prototypes, comprehensive portfolios, and public presentations evaluated by top Egyptian university professors.

Clean Water Solutions

Grade 11 students developing affordable water purification systems for rural communities

Renewable Energy

Grade 10 teams creating innovative solar and wind energy solutions for Egyptian villages

Final Exhibition

Teams present their work to a committee of top Egyptian university professors for evaluation

Classroom Experience

Innovative learning environments and teaching methods that inspire excellence

Unique Class System

Small Class Sizes

Maximum 24 students per class compared to 60-70 in most Egyptian schools, allowing for personalized attention and active participation.

Harkness Pedagogy

Students lead discussions around oval tables while teachers facilitate. This method develops critical thinking and communication skills.

Practical Sessions

Weekly laboratory sessions in fully-equipped science labs where students conduct experiments and develop hands-on skills.

Self-Directed Learning

Students research concepts in college-level references, present topics to peers, and suggest materials for deeper understanding.
