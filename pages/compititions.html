Historical Achievements

Our journey of excellence in international competitions

First International Medal

Our student won Egypt's first silver medal in the International Mathematical Olympiad, opening doors for future generations.

2014

Breakthrough in ISEF

First Egyptian team to win a Grand Award at Intel ISEF for innovative water purification technology.

2016

Robotics Championship

Our robotics team won the FIRST Robotics Competition Regional Championship, qualifying for world finals.

2018

Multiple Gold Medals

Historic achievement with 3 gold medals in IMO and 2 gold medals in IPhO in the same year.

2020

World Record Performance

Our students achieved perfect scores in multiple competitions, setting new records for Egyptian teams.
