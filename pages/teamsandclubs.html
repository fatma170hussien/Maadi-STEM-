Teams and Clubs

Where passion meets innovation at Maadi STEM High School

OUR TEAMS

MAADI STEM MUN

Maadi STEM Model United Nations

MAADI STEM MUN Activity

Our prestigious MUN team participates in national and international conferences, where students represent countries, debate global issues, and develop critical thinking, public speaking, research, and diplomacy skills. The team has earned numerous awards and recognition for outstanding performance.

TEDx MaadiSTEM

TEDx MaadiSTEM

TEDx MaadiSTEM Activity

The official TEDx license holders at Maadi STEM, organizing annual conferences that bring together thinkers, innovators, and creators. Students learn event planning, public speaking, marketing, and content creation while spreading impactful ideas within our community.

OUR CLUBS

Biology Club

A scientific community that explores life sciences through discussions, experiments, and collaborative learning beyond traditional classrooms. This club encourages hands-on discovery, from microscopic organisms to ecosystems, fostering curiosity and scientific thinking. Members engage in lab work, field studies, and projects related to genetics, ecology, and biotechnology, preparing them for future careers in research, medicine, and environmental science.

Geology Club

Dedicated to understanding earth sciences with a passion for rocks, minerals, and geological processes. The club organizes rock-hunting trips, mineral identification workshops, and discussions on topics like plate tectonics, volcanoes, and fossil records. It's an ideal space for students interested in how the Earth formed, changes over time, and the natural forces shaping our planet today.

English Club

A creative space for debates, writing, and discussions that strengthens communication and language confidence. Members participate in activities such as poetry slams, storytelling sessions, essay contests, and book clubs. The club helps improve public speaking, critical thinking, and expressive writing skills while exploring diverse literary genres and cultures.

Sports Club

Encouraging teamwork, fitness, and discipline through various physical and athletic activities. Whether through team sports like football and basketball or individual pursuits like running and yoga, this club promotes a healthy lifestyle, leadership, and sportsmanship. It's also a platform for organizing intramural competitions, fitness challenges, and wellness workshops.

Technology Club

Exploring coding, innovation, and emerging technologies to prepare students for the digital future. Members learn programming languages, develop software and apps, and experiment with concepts in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science. The club bridges theory with real-world tech solutions, inspiring innovation and problem-solving.

Media Club

Developing skills in photography, videography, and digital storytelling for modern media platforms. Students get hands-on experience in creating content, editing videos, managing social media, and producing podcasts or short films. The club emphasizes visual communication, creative expression, and technical proficiency in today's media-driven world.

Pathfinders

Develops leadership, outdoor skills, and community service through adventure activities, camping trips, and character-building exercises. More than just an outdoor club, Pathfinders teaches survival skills, teamwork, resilience, and empathy. Members participate in hikes, volunteer projects, and leadership workshops, fostering a spirit of service and exploration.

Code & Circuits

Combines programming with electronics to build interactive projects, from simple circuits to complex IoT devices and robotics systems. This club is perfect for those interested in hardware and software integration, offering practical experience with microcontrollers, sensors, and automation. Projects might include smart home systems, robots, or wearable tech.

Hack Club

Part of a global network of student-led coding clubs where members build projects, learn new technologies, and participate in hackathons. Focused on collaborative coding, innovation, and real-world problem-solving, Hack Club provides resources, mentorship, and a community for young developers to create, share, and compete in tech events.
